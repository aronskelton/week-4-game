Beat the Fuhrer

Instructions:


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Target Number"
  2. There are four attacks below.
    • flying kick
    • ray gun
    • jaguar
    • chainsaw
  3. By clicking on an attack, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the "Target Number".
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. The value of each attack is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each attack move.
